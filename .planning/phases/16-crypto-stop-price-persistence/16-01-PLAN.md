<objective>
Persist stop-loss and take-profit prices from the execution pipeline through FillProcessor into the positions table, closing the data flow gap that silently disables crypto stop enforcement.

Purpose: The PositionSizer computes stop/TP prices on SizedOrder, but FillProcessor only receives FillResult (which lacks these fields). The positions table has the columns (added in Phase 12) but they are always NULL, so stop_polling skips all crypto positions.

Output: FillProcessor writes stop_loss_price, take_profit_price, and side to positions on buy fills; carries them forward on partial sells; pipeline passes sized_order context to FillProcessor.
</objective>
